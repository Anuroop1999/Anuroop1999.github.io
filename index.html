About

I am a PhD candidate in Astronomy and Astrophysics working on the physical and structural evolution of protoplanetary disks and the detection of young substellar companions. My research spans the outer to inner disk, with a strong emphasis on eruptive young stars.

Research

  • Protoplanetary disk structure and evolution
  • High-contrast imaging of young stellar objects
  • Episodic accretion and FU Orionis outbursts
  • Optical and infrared interferometry

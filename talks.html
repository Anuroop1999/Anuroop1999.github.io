Beyond the academy

Outreach

  • Great Asteroid Festival (5th edition). AUI / NRAO / YEMS outreach stand at Las Vizcachas, Puente Alto, Chile (2026).
  • Colloquium host, ESO Chile. Organising and hosting the weekly colloquium programme (ongoing).
  • Exploring Exoplanets. A PME-funded project with the YEMS Millennium Nucleus, Astrophysics at School, developing an educational card game and activity book on exoplanets, disks, and Solar System moons for underserved communities (ongoing).
  • Public astronomy. Public telescope observations and guided eclipse viewing at Universidad Diego Portales, plus science communication and online seminars introducing astrophysics to students in India and Chile.
